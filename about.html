Est 2000

Company History

Magoche Drilling was founded in 2000 by Tarisai Tshuma, a seasoned driller with a passion for delivering high-quality drilling services. With a humble beginning and a single rig, the company started by serving local construction companies in Mberengwa, Masvingo, Felebusi and more.

Over the years, Magoche Drilling has grown steadily, expanding its fleet and expertise to become a leading drilling contractor in Zimbabwe Today, we pride ourselves on our commitment to safety, reliability, and customer satisfaction, with a team of experienced professionals dedicated to exceeding client expectations.
