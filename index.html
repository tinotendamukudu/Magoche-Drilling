Our Services

Below are Services offered by our company

Construction

Construction of borehole pads and surrounding infrastructure. Building of pump houses and control rooms in an efficient manner

Drilling Services

Drilling for water wells, foundations, and geotechnical applications. Expertise in various drilling methods (rotary, percussion, auger)

Borehole Repairs

Diagnosis and repair of borehole issues such as blockage, collapse and other issues. We also do rehabilitation of existing boreholes

Surveying and Siting

Site assessment and surveying for optimal borehole placement. We also provide GPS mapping and surveying services.

Capacity Testing

Pump testing and capacity assessment for water wells. We also specialise in testing water flow rate and its volume

Irrigation System Supply

We supply irrigation systems and equipment such as pumps, pipes and fittings. We also specialise in installation and maintenance of these systems
